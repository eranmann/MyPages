Popular Short Hikes in North Cascades National Park

Explore these beautiful trails, all under 4 miles long with less than 600 feet of elevation gain.

1. Thunder Knob Trail

Length: 3.6 miles round trip

Elevation gain: 425 feet

Features: Panoramic views of Diablo Lake and surrounding peaks

More information on NPS website

2. Rainy Lake Trail

Length: 2 miles round trip

Elevation gain: 70 feet

Features: Wheelchair accessible, ends at a scenic alpine lake

More information on NPS website

3. Washington Pass Overlook

Length: 0.5 miles round trip

Elevation gain: 200 feet

Features: Spectacular views of Liberty Bell Mountain and Early Winters Spires

More information on NPS website

4. Happy Creek Forest Walk

Length: 0.3 miles round trip

Elevation gain: Minimal

Features: Boardwalk trail through old-growth forest along Happy Creek

More information on NPS website

5. Sterling Munro Trail

Length: 0.5 miles round trip

Elevation gain: Minimal

Features: Wheelchair accessible boardwalk with views of Picket Range

More information on NPS website
